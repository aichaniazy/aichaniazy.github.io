Uber's Email Design System

As part of revamping Uber's Creative & Content Strategy, we created a new email design system aligned with industry best practices.  We design an advanced Figma component library using the atomic design method to maintain consistency. Our developers mirrored this library on the development side to ensure we deliver pixel-perfect communications.

Our modular design system is created with the messaging hierarchy in mind. Emails must not only be visually appealing and engaging, but they also need to convert.

We used figma’s best functionality to design our modules in a smart and efficient way that helps us speed up and streamline the design process thereby reducing turnaround times.
