Kill the jaguar shark
Design and art direction for an imaginary board game inspired by Wes Anderson. It is based on a real board game called Kill Dr. Lucky, as well as Anderson's The Life Aquatic With Steve Zissou. The aim of Kill The Jaguar Shark is to find the jaguar shark and kill him. The board game pieces were 3D printed taking the shape of Zissou's hat. An envelope was sent out to participating members, and it includes a submarine invitation and separate event tickets. And finally, merchandise for the fans, and a theme for the countdown website.
Adobe Awards semifinalist, 2017
Categories — Design & Art Direction - Print Production - Game Design
New perspectives
Large format (24x36in) poster design for an imaginary series of talks “New Perspectives”. Analog methods were employed to create the typography. The bottom left corner includes the details to attract the viewers to come close for more information.

Falcon smokers
This project involved developing the brand identity for Falcon Smokers, a company specializing in high-quality fire pits and BBQ smokers.
‍
The core of the brand identity is a versatile logo that ingeniously combines the imagery of a flickering flame with the silhouette of a falcon. Its asymmetrical design ensures visual integrity and correct orientation from any perspective, regardless of the product's placement or the viewer's position around the fire pit. This thoughtful design element enhances brand recognition and user experience.
