For over three years, we have dedicated our efforts to enhance Uber's customer relationship management (CRM) programs. Our work spans a diverse range of communication types, formats, and channels to effectively engage existing customers at key moments. This includes designing an email system, implementing in-app marketing, and crafting push notifications, SMS, and more. Additionally, we produce paid social content in over 20 languages to ensure global reach and relevance.
Dark Mode & Accessibility
Over several months, we rigorously tested color palettes across 11 different email clients to ensure brand accessibility in our email designs. Based on our findings, we developed a comprehensive design system, including a set of best practices and guidelines, to achieve optimal results.
Workshops
In 2022, we developed a comprehensive email design system for Uber and assisted with their rebranding efforts. Having earned their trust, Uber asked us to conduct workshops on strategy, brand guidelines, and tone of voice for sister agencies, aiming to unify all communications sent on their behalf.
