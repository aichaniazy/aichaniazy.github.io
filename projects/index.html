Partner up

I worked with myeatpal shortly after they launched the app. I started out as a product designer focusing on designing missing flows and enhancing the user experience, and then slowly delving into revamping the look and feel while building their figma design system from scratch.

Eventually, I became a consultant overseeing a senior product designer and social media designs, and brought on a motion design team to assist in creating a short reel explaining what myeatpal is.

Personalization at every step

In designing new user flows, we prioritized tailoring the experience to each user persona. We implemented dynamic features that provide real-time updates on the number of available dishes and restaurants as users adjust filters and specify dietary preferences.
